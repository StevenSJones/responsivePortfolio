About Me

My name is Steven Jones and I frequent Colorado's front roads and backstreets from Idaho Springs in the west to Akron in the east and from Windsor in the  north to Trinidad in the South and a lot of what is in between. I've plotted, built, and traversed these roads between 2005 and the current time. I was born in Boulder, CO and have lived in Aurora, Broomfield, Colorado Springs, Denver, Lafayette, and Westminster during my extensive time in the "Centennial  State."

My immediate family consists of my wife Moriah, Sons Steven and Ge'Ovonni and my daughters Teriah, Jada and Shayla, and mother-in-law Lori. I currently roam  the local terrain delivering hospice equipment as well as oxygen and other related items to those souls in need whether it be morning, noon, night, holiday, or  weekends. I enter people's lives at the point at which they or their loved ones begin or end their ultimate journey.

I spend all of my time while not at work or doing coding travelling the country; and honestly the world, with my family likey in a manner connected to my  youngest son Steven playing on his youth travel hockey team. I enjoy watching and playing ice hockey with my son as well as bowling, drawing, studying all matters related to the planetary sciences, baking with my wife, and staying active. I follow the Boston Bruins in the NHL, the Oakland Raiders in the NFL, the Los Angeles Clippers in the NBA and Liverpool in the Premier League.
